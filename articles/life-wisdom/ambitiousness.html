Indeed, it’s frustrating when you have multiple interests and can’t contain the immense chaos that manifests within yourself. Ambitiousness eventually teases us into considering something merely mediocre - like giving up our dreams and choose the normal life, because in the world we live in, anything that stands out from the ordinary is meant to be rejected — and/or labeled as a problem. The truth is, every ambitious person who has managed to channel their own strength into what they long for has become a revolutionary — and i don’t mean fame, because not everyone has at the tip of their tongue the name of the one who invented water filtration, or the one who gave birth to what we now call a book, among others.

And that leads me to conclude: when we have this killer urge to achieve something, it must be organized and cultivated — because — the same force that could create something visionary and change the world could also make us the most tormented of human beings, if ignored and buried deep within the soul.

So, the solution i find to escape mediocrity and poverty (not necessarily monetary) wouldn’t be to avoid pain, but to seek it — through the development of whatever constantly disturbs us, no matter what it is. The most important thing, to reach a life of fulfillment, is to create (instead of consume), no matter how small, silly, or stupid it may seem to most people — remember: the majority is condemned, so whatever you do to go the opposite direction, it counts. know that you are not alone — once you begin to stay consistent with ambition, you will find your kind, and that force will be amplified.
